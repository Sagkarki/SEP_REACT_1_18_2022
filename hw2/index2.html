Strategy

Mark’s a technical trader. He studies charts and looks for very specific patterns. Beyond that, there are three technical indicators Mark looks for in every single trade: volume, volatility, and catalysts. Another thing to know about Mark is that he has a negative bias — he likes to short stocks more than he likes to go long. Mark usually buys weekly put options to cap his risk and leverage his gains.

The Former Accountant’s Numbers

  • Started trading with $50,000 in 2010
  • Over $3 million in lifetime trading profits (and counting…)
  • Average gain of over $1,800 per trade
  • Average % gain of over 7.5% per trade
